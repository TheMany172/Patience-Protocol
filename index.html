The Patience Protocol: Requirements

This is a challenge of the mind. These five rules are obligatory. Modification to this is impermissible.

Requirement Focus Detail
1. Daily Exercise Steadfastness You must complete some form of exercise every single day for 115 consecutive days. The minimum suggested duration is 20 minutes, but the commitment is the key.
2. Five Moments of Reflection Focus You must pause 5 times daily to reflect on your path, check your mindset, and reaffirm your commitment: Wake-Up, Lunch, Afternoon, Evening, and Before Bed. It is recommended that you sit or kneel on the floor for a few minutes.
3. Read Discipline You must read some of a book. Audiobooks DO count.
4. Visual Tracking Accountability You must take note of your progress, however you want: photos, videos, etc. This could even just be tracking your workouts on your preferred platform.
5. Restraint Resolve Obviously you aren't allowed any alcohol or recreational drugs, why would that ever be a thing. Follow a diet that you think will improve upon your current one. Eat more fruit and veg, or less sugar, anything that is a tangible improvement
